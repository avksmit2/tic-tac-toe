Let's play Tic Tac Toe!

Rules:

  • Click "Start" to begin.
  • A player will be designated to be either an "X" or an "O".
  • Place your mark in a box.
  • Whoever gets three marks in a row, wins! This can be either horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
  • If there are no more moves to make an no one has three marks in a row, it is a tie.
  • Click "Start" to play a new game.
